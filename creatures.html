Organ Tree

A birch tree seen growing fruits on the branches. When a fruit is removed, the tree will begin rotting, and any fruits attached will rot until picked.

Known "Organs":

Heartfruit

Resembles a human heart. Has a red juice inside. Tastes vaguely of pork. Edible.

Lungapple

Resembles a human lung. Empty inside with inflated flexible skin. Tastes vaguely of pineapple. Edible.

Stomachcitrus

Resembles a human stomach. Filled with fertilized soil. Taste unknown. Inedible.

Crawlbag

Bug-like creature with mouths on the top and bottom of the main bulb. 4 legs protrude from the corners. The bottom mouth is used primarily for feeding on low foods (weeds and flowers), and the top mouth is used primarily for higher plants (fruits and vines).
